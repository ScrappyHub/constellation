# Constellation Architecture

Constellation organizes multiple ecosystems into a coherent digital civilization model.

## Architecture Overview

Constellation

├─ Covenant Systems
├─ Atlas Systems
├─ Fides Systems
├─ AION Systems
├─ Rooted
├─ Core
├─ Venture Labs
└─ Chronicle Systems

Each ecosystem has a distinct role within the broader architecture.

## Ecosystem Roles

### Covenant Systems
Governance, law, policy enforcement, trust, transport, evidence, and governed infrastructure.

### Atlas Systems
Recovery, restoration, artifacts, and historical continuity.

### Fides Systems
Proof instruments, verification systems, validation frameworks, and artifact integrity tools.

### AION Systems
Intelligence systems, policy‑aware AI, and machine reasoning architectures.

### Rooted
Community, civic, cultural, geographic, and vertical platforms.

### Core
Platform foundations and deterministic compute systems.

### Venture Labs
Experimental and commercial exploration platforms.

### Chronicle Systems
Gaming, interactive worlds, and digital preservation.

## Federation Model

Constellation operates as a federation rather than a monolith.

Each ecosystem remains sovereign and internally coherent while still fitting within the broader architectural structure.

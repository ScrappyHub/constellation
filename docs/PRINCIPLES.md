# Constellation Principles

## Sovereign Ecosystems
Each ecosystem maintains its own identity, scope, and internal architecture.

## Verifiable Architecture
Systems should be understandable, inspectable, and grounded in evidence.

## Clear Boundaries
Strong naming, explicit ownership, and legible architecture are preferred.

## Integration Through Demonstration
Systems integrate through demonstrated capability and architectural clarity.

## Long‑Horizon Design
Constellation is intended to support durable systems rather than short‑lived software.
